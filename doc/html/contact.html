Infomap LSA Contact Information

This software was developed by the Infomap project, which is part of the Computational Semantics Lab at the Center for the Study of Language and Information (CSLI) at Stanford University (whew!).

The code was written over time by Hinrich Shuetze, Stefan Kaufmann, Dominic Widdows, and Beate Dorow. It was prepared for public release by Scott Cederberg.

Questions regarding compilation difficulties, requests for features, and comments on the software should be e-mailed to infomap at csli dot stanford dot edu.

Thank you for using our code!
